I've been using LLM to help me write emails, capitons, video scripts, ideas, code, etc. For almost everyday for the past 3 years since the introduction of chatGPT.

Since then i've been trying and experimenting with other models from openai, antrophic, google, deepseek, moonshot, z.ai, glm, mistral, grok and more.

I switch around depending on the free tier limit and use them for the basics of tasks as simple as short email reply.

As fast and convenient as it is, I felt the need to do something. I don't want the delegate every single thinking, word, sentence, paragraphs and be completely reliant on a super amazing and complex 'linear algebra'.

I want to regain the originality and thought of writing. I want to have a REAL voice again.

So with this blog/article, I'm challenging my self to Re-Learn 'How to write'.

I won't fix any grammer or typo and watn to use mixed language formal-semiformal slang, any.

ofcourse mid writing this I realize, that technically I can put a lot of my 'original' writing and tell the LLM to learn from my writing style. BUTT i guess that's for a future project ;)
